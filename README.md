## Maze Generator
<img width="1511" height="779" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b45224c8-a59b-4e11-bcbf-f0c53f59b075" />

### What it does:
Generates mazes with a single solution (no loops) using a randomized recursive algorithm.

### Controls: 
Solve mazes with WASD, each input traverses the maze until reaching a junction or dead end and highlights every square it traversed. Whenever reaching the end of the maze, it instantly generates a new one.

### Time tracking: 
Your individual time to solve for every previous maze, your average time to solve, and your total time spent solving is displayed below the maze.
